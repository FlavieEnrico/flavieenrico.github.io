Swarm: What if climate deniers were poets?

Warning. The data below presents misinformation about global warming.

Abstract:

Swarm is a participatory research on climate denial, extending on the project Critical Climate Machine, an artistic environment designed the mechanisms of misinformation on global warming. Here, the result is a critical dataset in the form of a collection of haiku poetry.

The dataset is composed of 400 documents. It is divided into four parts. Each represents a set of 100 IDs of tweets recorded in the climatosceptic sphere, in French, Spanish and English. Given the policy of the Twitter platform, the original messages cannot be published. As a substitute, each reference is accompanied by a transformation of the original text in the form of a short poem, respecting the classic form of the haiku. One of the four parts of the dataset is rendered in the specific form of the Argentinian haiku.
